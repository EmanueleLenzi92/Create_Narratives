### The MOVING CSV file


This folder contains the CSV Excel file provided by the MOuntain Valorisation through INterconnectedness and Green growth (MOVING) (https://www.moving-h2020.eu/) Horizon 2020 European project. In this CSV file, MOVING collected data regarding 454 value chains of 16 european Country.



------------------------------------------------------
Structure of the CSV
------------------------------------------------------

The CSV file includes:

-454 rows of data (excluding the header). Each row provides information about a territory and represents a specific value chain.

-52 columns detailing various attributes related to the territories and value chains.



------------------------------------------------------
Data Categories
------------------------------------------------------

Data associated with the columns can be categorized into three main types:

1) Textual descriptions of natural characteristics of the territory (e.g. "Reference mountain landscape" and "Brief description of the main characteristics of the Mountain Reference Landscape" columns); 

2) Quantitative descriptions of the value chains (e.g. "Size of the area (km2)" and "Min Altimetry of LAU" columns); 

3) Specific attributes of the regional products (e.g. "Synthetic description of the value chain" and "Issues of interest to the MOVING project" columns)



===============================================================
        Dictionary of the CSV Columns
===============================================================

In the following, we report an explanation of the values for each column of the CSV file. The reported values are all in textual format.


Member State: Country where the value chain is located

Card ID: Unique identifier for each value chain entry

Descriptor of the value chain: Description of the specific value chain

Issues of interest to the MOVING project: Relevant issues for the MOVING project related to the value chain

Challenges of the VC in light of the MOVING project: Challenges faced by the value chain considering the MOVING project

Type of challenges: Nature of the challenges (Current or Predicted)

Type of VC: 'Classification of the value chain (Traditional or Innovative)

Type of Innovation: Source of innovation (Endogenous or Exogenous)

Brief description of the ‘innovation’ in the VC: Summary of the innovation present in the value chain

Innovation linked to: Aspects to which the innovation is related (Environmental, Social, Economic, etc.)

Reasons for selection : A description that explains why the value chain was selected in the project

Land use systems: Type of land of the value chain (Natural land, Grassland, Forestry, etc.)

Local Assets: Type of local asset of a value chain (Natural, Social, Cultural etc.)

Description of the key local asset(s): A description of the local assets of a value chain

Synthetic description of the value chain: Concise description of the value chain

Other information that may be considered of interest for the project: Additional relevant information for the MOVING project

Reference mountain chain: Mountain chain where the value chain is based

Reference mountain landscape: Specific mountain landscape related to the value chain

LAU: Local Administrative Unit code

Size of the area (km2): Area size in square kilometers

Min Altimetry of LAU: Minimum altitude of the Local Administrative Unit

Max Altimetry of LAU: Maximum altitude of the Local Administrative Unit

Total population (in thousand): Total population of the area in thousands

Population density (Inhabitants/km2) [LAU]: Population density per square kilometer in the Local Administrative Unit

Population change rate of LAU in the last 10 years (%): Population change rate in the Local Administrative Unit over the last decade

Regional population density (Inhabitants/km2): Population density per square kilometer in the region

Regional population change rate in the last 10 years (%): Population change rate in the region over the last decade

Average per capita income (EUR)/year [NUTS3]: Average annual per capita income in Euros at NUTS3 level

Total Gross Value Added (GVA) (EUR million)/year [NUTS3]: Total Gross Value Added per year in million Euros at NUTS3 level

Primary sector share of total GVA/year [NUTS3]: Share of the primary sector in total Gross Value Added per year at NUTS3 level

Secondary sector share of total GVA/year [NUTS3]: Share of the secondary sector in total Gross Value Added per year at NUTS3 level

Tertiary sector share of total GVA/year [NUTS3]: Share of the tertiary sector in total Gross Value Added per year at NUTS3 level

Primary sector share of total employment/year [NUTS3]: Share of the primary sector in total employment per year at NUTS3 level

Secondary sector share of total employment/year [NUTS3]: Share of the secondary sector in total employment per year at NUTS3 level

Tertiary sector share of total employment/year [NUTS3]: Share of the tertiary sector in total employment per year at NUTS3 level

Per capita income (EUR)/year [NUTS2]: Average annual per capita income in Euros at NUTS2 level

Total Gross Value Added (GVA) (EUR million)/year [NUTS2]: Total Gross Value Added per year in million Euros at NUTS2 level

Primary sector share of total GVA/year [NUTS2]: Share of the primary sector in total Gross Value Added per year at NUTS2 level

Secondary sector share of total GVA/year [NUTS2]: Share of the secondary sector in total Gross Value Added per year at NUTS2 level

Tertiary sector share of total GVA/year [NUTS2]: Share of the tertiary sector in total Gross Value Added per year at NUTS2 level

Primary sector share of total employment/year [NUTS2]: Share of the primary sector in total employment per year at NUTS2 level

Secondary sector share of total employment/year [NUTS2]: Share of the secondary sector in total employment per year at NUTS2 level

Tertiary sector share of total employment/year [NUTS2]: Share of the tertiary sector in total employment per year at NUTS2 level

Total bed places (BPs) in tourist accommodations/year [LAU]: Total number of bed places in tourist accommodations per year in the Local Administrative Unit

Regional (NUTS2) share of total BPs/year: Regional share of total bed places per year at NUTS2 level

Number of agricultural holdings [LAU]: Total number of agricultural holdings in the Local Administrative Unit

Nearest settlement with population > 100000 (km): Distance to the nearest settlement with a population greater than 100,000

Presence of Protected Areas': Indicates whether there are protected areas in the region

Brief description of the main characteristics of the Mountain Reference Landscape: Summary of the main characteristics of the referenced mountain landscape

District / Sub-region: District or sub-region where the value chain is located

NUTS 3: NUTS 3 classification code

NUTS 2: NUTS 2 classification code
